## FAQ

**Does it show the exact satellites my phone is locked onto?**
No. iOS doesn't expose the receiver's raw satellite data. Your position and fix
accuracy are real (Core Location); the satellites shown are calculated from
public orbital data (TLE/SGP4) to be above your horizon.

**Why does it need my location?**
To place satellites on the radar relative to you and compute which are overhead.
Your location is never stored or shared.

**How do I manage or cancel Pro?**
On iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → GPS Test Satellite Tracker.

**How do I restore a purchase?**
Open the app → subscription screen → "Restore Purchases".
